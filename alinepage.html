Description

A-line gowns generally feature a fitted bodice with a skirt that gradually flares from the hips, loosely resembling a narrow “A” shape. Also widely flattering, A-line dresses have been worn by many notable celebrities on their wedding day, including Allison Williams, Chanel Iman, and Gwyneth Paltrow. Though not quite as dramatic, A-line dresses are slightly more versatile than ball gowns, as there’s often more flexibility with fabrics and necklines.
